What is an Adjective?

An ADJECTIVE is a word that DESCRIBES a noun.

Adjectives tell us MORE about the noun.

Without adjective:

I saw a dog.

With adjectives:

I saw a big, brown, friendly dog.

Adjectives help us PICTURE the noun! 🎨

Learn

Adjectives Answer THREE Questions

1
What kind? — red apple, happy child, cold water (describes type or quality)
2
How many? — five dogs, many people, some books (tells number or amount)
3
Which one? — this desk, that car, those shoes (points to a specific noun)

💡 Most adjectives answer "What kind?"

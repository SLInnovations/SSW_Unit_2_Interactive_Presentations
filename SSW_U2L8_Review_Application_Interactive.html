Unit 2 Final Review

Click each card to reveal the answer.

What do adjectives describe?
Nouns (people, places, things, animals). They tell us what kind, how many, and which one.
What do adverbs describe?
Verbs (actions). They tell us how, when, where, and to what extent.
What is the comma rule for adjectives?
Use commas between adjectives when you can put "and" between them.
"tall, green tree" (tall AND green ✓)
When do you use A vs. AN?
A before consonant sounds. AN before vowel sounds. Listen to the SOUND, not the letter.
What does "overloading" mean?
Using too many describing words. Keep it balanced: 2–3 adjectives per noun, 1–2 adverbs per verb. Quality over quantity.
What words should you avoid?
Weak, vague words: really, very, good, nice, big, fun. Replace with specific, vivid words.
